Project Objective: 
To develop an automated narrative analysis tool that assists authors and editors by evaluating text drafts. The system will generate quantifiable metrics and actionable feedback on structural and stylistic elements (e.g., readability, pacing, and character distribution), thereby streamlining the editing process and improving manuscript quality without replacing human creative judgment.
The subjective part such as the idea, the feeling and how close to the reader the texts get cannot be analyzed. What can be analyzed is the objective part like the grammar, the repetition, the structure, character usage, different rules of writing.
Stakeholders:
Authors (writers seeking to improve their drafts)
Editors (professionals needing a tool to speed up their review and critique process)
The Publishing Company (management/owners who want to publish higher-quality books more efficiently and reduce editorial costs)
The Development Team (who will build the system)
